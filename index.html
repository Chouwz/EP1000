EP 1000 Digital Fabrication

Digital Fabrication - Module Aims :

In this elective, we are introduced to design thinking concepts, which we will use to design some functional prototypes to meet certain user requirements. Fundamental digital fabrication skills and technologies such as computer-aided design, 2D and 3D modeling, mechanical & electronic design and programming skills are developed through a range of hands-on real world activities. Project management, web and version control techniques, which are important for project development, will also be imparted to us in this elective.
